Resources

Here are some quick links to my notes, formula sheets, and course reviews from my time in university. If you want to learn more about engineering physics, check out engphys.ca and the Atom Magazine for Engineering Physics.

Page Description
Medical School Resources This page contains resources that I have compiled from my time in medical school at the University of Calgary. These include notes, CaRMS R-1 match rate analysis, and CMA specialty profile summaries.
USMLE Tips This page contains my experience with the USMLE from a Canadian med student perspective from a 3-year program at the University of Calgary.
Notes and Formula Sheets This page contains a collection of my notes and formula sheets from courses I've taken at the University of Alberta. Feel free to use, share, and modify at your leisure. Please keep in mind that these notes were designed to provide a quick reference during examinations, so please be aware of things that your course may include that I haven't included in my notes. All pdfs were typeset using LaTeX unless XeLaTeX was specified in the preamble of the .tex file.
Course Reviews This page contains a collection of 40+ courses that I've taken in my neuroscience and engineering physics programs and my review of the course materials. For each course, I include a description of the course and tips for doing well. For select courses, I give a shoutout to professors who went above and beyond in teaching.
Getting Involved in Research This page contains a guide on getting involved in research as someone with little to no prior research experiences. This will be a general guide for all types of research (e.g., basic science, computational research, biomedical engineering research).
